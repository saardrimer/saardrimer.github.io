Hello.

Welcome.

I'm Saar Drimer and this is my homepage.

I'm an engineer with an appreciation for design, art, culture, and how they're all combined into the things that I and others create. My greatest fear is to bore myself or be considered boring by others.

I mostly create through Boldport, my company. Examples of my work are here, and some of it can be purchased here.

LinkedIn has the dry facts about my career so far; I kind-of look good on paper. I'm available for — and interested in! — contracting work and commissions. Please get in touch so we can discuss how I may be able to help.

Here's some more about me:

  • For three years I ran a service called Boldport Club where I sent unique electronics soldering projects that I designed every month to over 500 people all over the world. The Club community is marvellous and lives on Discord. You can always find me there.
  • I created PCBmodE to allow me to realise my desire to combine circuit-boards, art, and coding. It's open source software written in Python and I'd love to continue developing it.
  • For a few years I was an academic, working on hardware and banking security. Stuff from those days is here.
  • I'm a compulsive doodler, not a morning person, and I really enjoy cooking.
  • My name means storm in Hebrew.

If you'd like to informally support my work you could buy me coffee or email me — saardrimer@gmail.com or saar@boldport.com — with words.
